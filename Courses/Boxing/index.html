Benefits Of Boxing Workouts

Boxing workouts aren’t just useful for amateur and professional boxers – anyone can benefit from this style of training! Some of the benefits include:

  • Increased speed and agility
  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Total body workout

What Kind Of Exercises Should A Boxing Workout Routine Include?

A good boxing workout routine should combine a few different kinds of exercises:

  • Boxing drills for speed and coordination
  • Cardiovascular exercises for endurance
  • Strength exercises for power
  • Core exercises for stability and punch strength
