About TRX

TRX (Total Resistance Exercises) is a revolutionary workout method that leverages your body weight and gravity to build strength, balance, flexibility, and core stability simultaneously. It's suitable for all fitness levels, offering scalable exercises that can be adjusted to suit beginners and advanced athletes alike.

Benefits of TRX Training

  • Improves core strength and stability.
  • Increases flexibility and balance.
  • Scalable for all fitness levels.
  • Engages multiple muscle groups simultaneously.
  • Portable and easy to set up anywhere.

TRX Exercises

TRX Squats

Enhance your lower body strength by performing squats with the assistance of TRX straps.

TRX Push-Ups

Build upper body strength with push-ups while maintaining core stability using TRX straps.

TRX Rows

Work on your back muscles by pulling your body toward the anchor point using TRX straps.

Sample TRX Routine

Try this beginner-friendly routine:

  • 10 TRX Squats
  • 10 TRX Push-Ups
  • 10 TRX Rows
  • 10 TRX Lunges
  • Repeat 3 times
